## What is this?

PassForge takes the personal info people commonly turn into passwords вҖ” names, birthdays, nicknames, pets, partners, kids вҖ” and **exhaustively generates every realistic combination** an attacker (or you) might try.

Use it to:
- **Test your own passwords** вҖ” see if yours appear in the list
- **Recover a forgotten password** you know was based on personal info
- **Security awareness** вҖ” show people why `Alex1998!` is not a strong password

Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is ever transmitted anywhere.

## Privacy

PassForge was designed from the ground up to never touch a server.

- вң… No form submissions
- вң… No `fetch()` or `XMLHttpRequest` to any backend
- вң… No localStorage or sessionStorage
- вң… No analytics (no GA, no Plausible, nothing)
- вң… No cookies
- вң… Fonts loaded from Google Fonts CDN *(remove the `<link>` tag for full offline use)*

To verify: open DevTools вҶ’ Network tab вҶ’ generate passwords вҶ’ watch zero requests fire.
